# FlaChat
This is one of my hobby projects, that is actually coming to life.
FlaChat (aka Flask Chat) is a chatting website based on "rooms". Each room is temporary.
When every person leaves, the room is gone... unless someone stays 24/7 doing nothing but nothing. A bot I would say.
It's simple. You put your nickname, and the room code... and boom. Your in. Or create a room with the "create room" button.
And I resumed development after being abandoned for almost A YEAR. You heard that right. Or, maybe almost a year.
